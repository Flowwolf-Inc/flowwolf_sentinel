## 7. Autonomy Credit System (Instead of Discounts)

**Problem**: Price discounts erode long-term value  
**Solution**: Non-monetary credits that unlock value faster

### Credit Types

#### Evidence Credits
**What**: Fewer data samples needed to level up  
**Example**: FDPs need 30 days L2 performance (vs 90 days standard)  
**Earn by**: Early adoption, clean data, partnership

#### Authority Credits
**What**: Higher auto-execution caps  
**Example**: L3 customer gets L4 confidence threshold (95% vs 90%)  
**Earn by**: Consistent performance, executive sponsorship

#### Velocity Credits
**What**: Priority enablement and reviews  
**Example**: 1-week autonomy review (vs 2-week standard)  
**Earn by**: Strategic partnership, referrals

### Credit Expiration
- Evidence Credits: 12-month validity
- Authority Credits: Tied to performance (revoked if dips)
- Velocity Credits: Per-upgrade basis

**Result**: Customers chase credits, not discounts. Pricing integrity maintained.
